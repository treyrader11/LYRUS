Lyrus is a single-page web app aggregate which fuses resources from YouTube, MusiXmatch, and Wikipedia, aiming to facilitate the process of searching for music and music related information. Lyrus makes it worth your while, whether users are seeking biographical information regarding an artist, wish to watch popular music videos, look up lyrics, or simultaneously want to do it all.
